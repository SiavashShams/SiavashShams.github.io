About Me

I am a B.Sc. graduate of the University of Tehran Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. Throughout my academic career, I have deepened my knowledge and skills in mathematics and fundamentals of artificial intelligence with applications to biomedical sciences and control systems. I also enjoy teaching since it allows me to consolidate my knowledge. To that end, I have volunteered as a Teaching assistant in a variety of AI-related courses. Moreover, I have participated in various research projects, which have resulted in a few publications.
