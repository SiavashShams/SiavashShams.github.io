Life Outside Academia

When I'm not immersed in research, I'm out on the field enjoying my favorite sports. I'm a soccer enthusiast and a huge fan of FC Barcelona, even though they are going through a tough phase right now 😁. I actively participate in various sports, including badminton, volleyball, swimming, and I hit the gym regularly to stay fit.

Overall, I believe in leading a balanced and fulfilling life, combining my academic pursuits with a vibrant and active lifestyle. This balance helps me stay motivated and inspired, both personally and professionally.
