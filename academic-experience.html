Research Experience

  • Research Assistant at Advanced Control Systems Laboratory January 2022 - Ongoing
    Supervised by: Prof. Mohammad Javad Yazdanpanah

    • In this research, our team focus is on controlling complex networks. We developed a comprehensive framework for optimal control of complex networks. And we seek to improve it further. This framework consists of optimal driver node selection in order to achieve controllability of the network and choosing the method of calculation of the optimal input regarding the type of the optimization problem. We tested our framework for desynchronization of the brain networks in order to stop epileptic seizures, and we submitted a paper to ICBME2022. We are currently working on optimal driver node selection with a convolutional neural network approach.

  • Research Assistant at Advanced Robotics and Intelligent Systems Laboratory June 2022 - September 2022
    Supervised by: Prof. Hadi Moradi

    • In this research, we designed a comprehensive platform for upper limb rehabilitation. We aim to deliver a Human-Computer Interaction form of treatment using gamification methods and a low-cost, efficient, and specific movement data acquisition system so as to motivate patients to increase their range of motion and make practices more exciting for them. Moreover, we conducted experiments to evaluate our designed platform, which yielded promising results. We submitted our findings to ICROM2022.

  • Research Intern at IoT Lab at University of Tehran June 2021 - September 2021
    Supervised by: Dr. Mehdi Modarressi

    • In this internship, my role was to design a dashboard on the ThingsBoard IoT platform and simulate a smart house. The data transferring was done via MQTT protocols. For further information, see my GitHub repository.
